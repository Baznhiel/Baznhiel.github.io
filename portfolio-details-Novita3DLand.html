Novita 3D Land

  • Category: Windows PC Game
  • Project date: 18 May, 2020

Action-Adventure Game

This is an action and adventure game, mixed with a bit of puzzles that I developed together with Francisco Rodrigo Vercher for the subjects of artificial intelligence and video game engines for the third year of the HND of video game programming at ESAT.

The game consists of completing puzzles until you reach the final bomb, activate it and be able to return to the beginning of the level before the time runs out.
